4. Market Assumptions (sensitivity-tested)

Your model must stress-test assumptions because crypto is volatile.

Consider:
	•	Token price scenarios (bull/base/bear)
	•	Bitcoin + ETH correlation impact on treasury
	•	Exchange volume assumptions
	•	AUM growth based on historical crypto cycles
	•	VC/institutional market appetite

You should model 3–5 scenarios, not just one.
