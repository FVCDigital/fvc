# 🚀 FVC Protocol Roadmap

## **OVERVIEW**
This document outlines the strategic roadmap for the FVC Protocol, from initial fundraising through full protocol maturity with governance, SME operations, and staking mechanisms.

---

## **🎯 STAGE 0: FUNDRAISING & AUDIT (CURRENT)**

### **Purpose**
- **Primary Goal**: Raise funds for professional smart contract audit
- **Secondary Goal**: Establish initial community and validate market demand
- **Duration**: Until audit funding target is reached

### **Mechanics**
- **Bonding Round 0**: USDC → FVC tokens with dynamic discount pricing
- **Allocation**: 20.5% of total FVC supply (2.05M FVC)
- **Vesting Period**: 90 days from bonding completion
- **Target**: Sufficient USDC to cover professional audit costs

### **Success Criteria**
- ✅ Audit funding target reached
- ✅ Professional audit completed
- ✅ Security vulnerabilities addressed
- ✅ Community established

### **Timeline**
- **Start**: Immediate (current)
- **Duration**: 2-4 weeks (or until target reached)
- **End**: Audit completion and Round 0 vesting unlock

---

## **🔓 STAGE 1: VESTING UNLOCK & LIQUIDITY (POST-ROUND 0)**

### **What Happens**
- **Vesting Unlock**: Round 0 participants receive their FVC tokens
- **Governance Activation**: FVC holders can participate in protocol decisions
- **DEX Trading**: FVC becomes tradeable on decentralized exchanges
- **Liquidity Formation**: Initial trading pairs established

### **Key Activities**
1. **Vesting Completion**: 90-day lock period ends
2. **Token Distribution**: FVC tokens transferred to bonded users
3. **DEX Listing**: FVC/USDC and FVC/ETH pairs created
4. **Liquidity Mining**: Incentivize liquidity providers
5. **Price Discovery**: Market determines FVC value

### **Success Criteria**
- ✅ All Round 0 participants receive FVC tokens
- ✅ FVC listed on major DEXs (Uniswap, SushiSwap, etc.)
- ✅ Sufficient liquidity for trading
- ✅ Stable price discovery

### **Timeline**
- **Start**: Round 0 vesting period ends
- **Duration**: 2-4 weeks
- **End**: Stable trading environment established

---

## **🗳️ STAGE 2: GOVERNANCE & COMMUNITY BUILDING**

### **Purpose**
- **Establish DAO Structure**: Community-driven decision making
- **Protocol Parameters**: Community votes on key decisions
- **Treasury Management**: Community controls raised funds
- **Strategic Planning**: Plan SME launch and future development

### **Governance Mechanisms**
1. **Proposal System**: FVC holders can submit proposals
2. **Voting Power**: 1 FVC = 1 vote
3. **Quorum Requirements**: Minimum participation thresholds
4. **Execution**: Timelock and multi-sig security

### **Key Governance Decisions**
- **Audit Results**: How to address any findings
- **Treasury Allocation**: Use of raised USDC funds
- **SME Strategy**: Business model and market approach
- **Protocol Upgrades**: Technical improvements and features
- **Partnerships**: Strategic alliances and integrations

### **Success Criteria**
- ✅ DAO governance structure operational
- ✅ Community actively participating in decisions
- ✅ Treasury management established
- ✅ Clear roadmap for SME launch

### **Timeline**
- **Start**: Post-vesting unlock
- **Duration**: 4-8 weeks
- **End**: SME launch strategy finalized

## **💰 STAGE 4: STAKING & REWARDS**

### **Purpose**
- **Token Utility**: Provide additional use cases for FVC
- **Revenue Sharing**: Distribute SME profits to stakers
- **Long-term Alignment**: Incentivize long-term holding
- **Protocol Growth**: Sustainable token economics

### **Staking Mechanisms**
1. **Flexible Staking**: Stake/unstake anytime
2. **Locked Staking**: Higher rewards for longer commitments
3. **Revenue Sharing**: Direct distribution of SME profits
4. **Governance Bonuses**: Additional rewards for active participants

### **Reward Structure**
- **Base APY**: From protocol operations
- **Performance Bonuses**: Based on SME loan performance
- **Governance Rewards**: For active community participation
- **Liquidity Incentives**: For providing DEX liquidity

### **Success Criteria**
